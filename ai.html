Applications of AI in Computer Science

1. How AI Works

Artificial Intelligence (AI) works by simulating aspects of human intelligence such as learning, reasoning, and problem-solving. It relies on algorithms, data, and computational models like neural networks to recognize patterns and make predictions. Machine learning, a subset of AI, enables systems to improve their performance over time as they are exposed to more data.

2. How AI is Useful

AI has numerous applications across computer science. It powers search engines, recommendation systems, natural language processing, robotics, and cybersecurity. Developers use AI to optimize algorithms, detect anomalies, and enhance decision-making processes. Its adaptability makes it a cornerstone of modern computing, supporting fields ranging from healthcare technology to financial forecasting.
